I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Physics and Astronomy of Sejong University in Seoul, South Korea. I mainly work on the dynamics of neutral hydrogen (HI) gas in galaxies in the local Universe (within ~10 Mpc), with a focus on gas inflows and outflows, circular and non-circular motions, and their connection to star formation and the baryon-dark matter interplay.

Research Interests

  • HI gas kinematics via multi-Gaussian profile decomposition
  • HI galaxy surveys with SKA-Mid/-precursor/-pathfinder radio telescopes
  • Cold-mode gas accretion in nearby galaxies
  • Dark matter distribution in dwarf galaxies and tests of ΛCDM simulations on galactic scales
